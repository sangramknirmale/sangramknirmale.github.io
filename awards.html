Awards and Achievements

Best Presentation award in Sustainable Transport Planning, Policies and Emerging Technologies Track at 14th Conference on Transportation Planning and Implementation Methodologies for Developing Countries (TPMDC-2022) for the paper titled “The adverse impact of headway variability on Bus Transit Ridership: Evidence from Bengaluru, India”.
  2. Best Paper award in the 14th Conference on Transportation Planning and Implementation Methodologies for Developing Countries (TPMDC-2022) for the paper titled “The adverse impact of headway variability on Bus Transit Ridership: Evidence from Bengaluru, India”.

Best paper award in Planning and Demand Modelling Track at 6th Conference of Transportation Research Group of India (CTRG-2021) for the paper titled “A direct demand model of bus transit ridership considering spatial disaggregation, demand-supply endogeneity, non-linear service response, accessibility measures, and inter-route relationships”.

  6. First rank in the exam conducted during the AICTE-sponsored short-term training program “Modelling and Control of Traffic under Mixed Conditions” organised by the Department of Civil Engineering, IIT Madras, from 2nd to 7th December 2019.
  7. First prize in a coding competition conducted during the GIAN course “Sampling for Signal Reconstruction vs Numerical Integration: Theory and Practice” organised by the Centre for Continuing Education, Indian Institute of Science, Bengaluru, India, from 27th to 31st August 2018.
