Awards and Achievements

  1. Young Faculty Award by Indian Institute of Technology Bombay in 2024
  2. Best Thesis Award in the traffic engineering area by the Transportation Research Group of India (TRG India) in 2023.
  3. Best Presentation award in Sustainable Transport Planning, Policies and Emerging Technologies Track at 14th Conference on Transportation Planning and Implementation Methodologies for Developing Countries (TPMDC-2022) for the paper titled “The adverse impact of headway variability on Bus Transit Ridership: Evidence from Bengaluru, India”. Note: I made this presentation.
  4. Best Paper award in the 14th Conference on Transportation Planning and Implementation Methodologies for Developing Countries (TPMDC-2022) for the paper titled “The adverse impact of headway variability on Bus Transit Ridership: Evidence from Bengaluru, India”. Note: I was a co-author on this paper.
  5. Best paper award in Planning and Demand Modelling Track at 6th Conference of Transportation Research Group of India (CTRG-2021) for the paper titled “A direct demand model of bus transit ridership considering spatial disaggregation, demand-supply endogeneity, non-linear service response, accessibility measures, and inter-route relationships”. Note: I was a co-author on this paper.
  6. Awarded with COMSNETS Fellowship for 2021 issued by International Conference on COMmunication Systems and NETworkS (COMSNETS).
  7. Best paper award in Traffic Flow Analysis and Facilities Design Track at 5th Conference of Transportation Research Group of India (CTRG-2019) for the paper titled “Driver Behaviour Models with Perception Errors: A choice Modelling Framework with Stochastic Variables”. Note: I was a lead author on this paper.
  8. First rank in the exam conducted during the AICTE-sponsored short-term training program “Modelling and Control of Traffic under Mixed Conditions” organised by the Department of Civil Engineering, IIT Madras, from 2nd to 7th December 2019.
