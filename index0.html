Welcome to my webpage!
I am an Assistant Professor at Department of Civil Engineering at Indian Institute of Technology Bombay. Previously, I worked as research scientist in the Centre for infrastructure, Sustainable Transportation and Urban Planning (CiSTUP) at the Indian Institute of Science (IISc), Bengaluru for one year. My research interests lie in the mathematical modelling of user behaviour in transportation systems, and I specialize in modelling driver behaviour using a discrete-continuous econometric framework and statistical methods. Please send me an email if you are interested in my work or collaboration.
