Welcome to my webpage!
I am an Assistant Professor in the Department of Civil Engineering at Indian Institute of Technology Bombay. My work is dedicated to transforming the future of urban infrastructure through innovative transportation systems. Previously, I served as a research scientist at the Centre for Infrastructure, Sustainable Transportation and Urban Planning (CiSTUP) at Indian Institute of Science (IISc), Bengaluru. There, I explored sustainable solutions for complex urban planning challenges. My research focuses on the mathematical modeling of user behavior in transportation systems, specializing in modeling driver behavior with a discrete-continuous econometric framework and statistical methods. My goal is to improve traffic flow and safety in diverse urban environments. I invite you to reach out for potential collaborations or to learn more about my work. Let's connect and discuss innovative approaches to shaping the future of transportation!
